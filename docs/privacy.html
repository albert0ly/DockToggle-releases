Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data collection

DockToggle does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The application makes no network connections of any kind.

Permissions

DockToggle requests Accessibility permission solely to observe and intercept Dock icon click events. This permission is not used for any other purpose. No screen contents, keystrokes, or other accessibility data are read or stored.

Analytics and telemetry

DockToggle contains no analytics, crash reporting, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.

Third parties

DockToggle does not integrate with any third-party services.

Contact

Questions or concerns can be raised via GitHub Issues.
