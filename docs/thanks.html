Thank you for downloading DockToggle!

Follow these steps to complete the installation.

1

Open DockToggle.dmg

Find DockToggle.dmg in your Downloads folder and double-click to open it.

2

Drag DockToggle to Applications

In the window that opens, drag DockToggle onto the Applications folder. Click Replace if asked.

3

Remove quarantine flag

Open Terminal (press ⌘ Space, type Terminal, press Enter) and run this command:

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/DockToggle.app
4

Open DockToggle from Launchpad

Open Launchpad (F4 or pinch on trackpad) and click DockToggle. Its icon will appear in your menu bar (top-right).

5

Grant Accessibility permission

DockToggle opens System Settings automatically. Toggle DockToggle ON in the Accessibility list.

Upgrading from a previous version? If DockToggle already appears with the toggle ON — toggle it OFF then back ON to re-link the permission to the new version.
🎉

You're all set!

Click any focused app's Dock icon — it hides and focus returns to your previous app. Exactly like Windows.
